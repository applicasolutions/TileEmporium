Return Policy

At Tile Emporium, we strive to provide our customers with the best possible products and services. While we take great care to ensure that our products are of the highest quality, we understand that there may be occasions where a customer may need to return a product. However, as a small startup, we currently do not offer returns on any products once they have been shipped. We encourage our customers to carefully review their orders before making a purchase.

In case of any defects or damages in the products, we offer a 48-hour window for customers to notify us. We will then work with the customer to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

We are always looking for ways to improve our business and would appreciate any feedback that our customers may have. If you have any suggestions on how we can improve our products or services, please do not hesitate to contact us.
